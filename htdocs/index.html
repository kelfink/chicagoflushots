Learn More

Welcome to the Chicago flu shot finder. If your device has GPS, you may click on the Find Me button to see locations near you. You may enter your address and click on Go to do the same. You may have to click on the Menu button first, on a tablet or smartphone. Click on All, Next 7 Days, or On A Day of the week to highlight locations available on a certain day. Blue map locations offer free flu shots for anyone six months of age or older.

Find out more information about flu shots from the Chicago Department of Public Health's website.



No Cost Flu Shots

Locations marked with a blue icon are Chicago Department of Public Health (CDPH) clinic events. There is no cost to individual. If insured, insurance will be billed.
